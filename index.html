About Eiso Chan / Trần Vĩnh Thông / 陈永聪 / 陳永聰 / 陳永聡 / 진영총 / 𛊝𛋟𛉱 / 𘔜𗍾𗢱

Eiso Chan borned in the actual Cat's Cradle, which the former place name is Quảng Châu Loan during the French Indochina period, majored in Chinese dialectology and linguistics in university, now works in the Culture and Art Publishing House in Beijing, China, concerns the Unicode encoding works for different living and historical scripts used in East Asia and Southeast Asia, and how to use the OpenType method to make information processing better and better.
